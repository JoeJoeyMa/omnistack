# Service Catalog Sources

This storefront now uses API-backed service products across four groups:

1. Company formation and compliance
2. Business accounts and treasury
3. Website, commerce, and deployment delivery
4. AI, support, and automation systems

The catalog copy is split into two layers:

1. Source-derived workflows and included items from public reference pages.
2. Maple Global bundled additions for delivery planning, compliance, deployment, handoff, and recurring operating support so the frontend can represent complete purchasable service modules.
